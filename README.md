## 👨‍🎓 About the Project

This website marks my first step into web development and is hosted permanently and for free via **GitHub Pages**. My goal is to continuously maintain and update this site throughout my studies, adding new academic projects, coursework, and practical skills.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack Used:
* **HTML5:** For page structure and semantic content.
* **CSS3:** For responsive design, modern layout, and styling.
* **GitHub Pages:** For seamless hosting and automated deployment.

## 📂 Website Structure

The site follows a clean, single-page layout including:
1. **Home / Bio:** A brief introduction of who I am and my academic focus.
2. **Education:** Academic details regarding my department at the University of Peloponnese.
3. **Skills:** Core technical and interpersonal skills I am currently developing.
4. **Contact:** Quick access to my Email and LinkedIn profiles.
